Usada Pekora

Achievements

  • Pekora's debut stream took place on 17 July 2019.
  • She was one of the top-10 most-watched female gaming livestreamers in Q3 2022.
  • In Q1 2023, she was the most-watched female streamer.
  • In August 2023, she was added as a playable character in the Hololive fangame Idol Showdown.
  • She was the most watched female streamer of 2023 by watch hours.

Activity

୨୧ Pekora's debut stream took place on 17 July 2019. Writing for Fortune, Nicholas Gordon described her as best known for streaming Japanese role-playing games, Minecraft prank content, and her friendship with fellow Hololive member Moona Hoshinova. On 13 December 2020, in celebration of reaching 1,000,000 YouTube subscribers, she streamed herself revealing her job as a virtual YouTuber to her mother.

୨୧ In May 2022, she went on hiatus due to voice-related health concerns. She returned in June 2022.

୨୧ In Q3 2022, Pekora was one of the top-10 most-watched female gaming livestreamers. In Q1 2023, she was the most-watched female streamer

୨୧ For the year of 2023, Pekora launched her promotional theme, the Human Rabbitality Project. Nicknamed the Rabbit Plan, this was to coincide with the Year of the Rabbit.

୨୧ On 11 August 2023, she was added as a playable character in the Hololive fangame Idol Showdown.

୨୧ In Dragon Quest Treasures there is a Killing Machine monster named Pekotte that is voiced and based on her. She can be unlocked by entering the code PEK in the gift code menu.

୨୧ According to streaming data analysis site Stream Charts, Pekora was the most watched female streamer of 2023 by watch hours.
